RvSnoop @version@ Developer’s Guide

Welcome to the RvSnoop developer’s guide! This guide gives an overview of the workings of the application, and the steps needed to build it from source and to submit modifications. The guide is comprised of several main sections:

  1. Downloading & Building From Source
  2. Coding Standards
  3. Records, Subjects, & Types
  4. The Record Ledger
  5. Importing & Exporting Records
  6. Other Software Used in RvSnoop

  1. FindBugs
  2. Lint4J
  3. PMD
