Other Software Used in RvSnoop

Various other open source prjects are used by RvSnoop, this chapter gives a brief introduction to each of them and how they are used.

Glazed Lists

Glazed Lists is used extensively throughout RvSnoop, it’s main use is as the basis for the record ledger where it provides sorting and filtering capabilities. Glazed Lists is also used to provide the connection list and the lists of recent projects and connections (the connection list is in fact a filtered view of the recent connections list).
